Chant Therapy and Satellite Data

What is Chant Therapy?

Chant therapy is a scientifically backed technique that uses vocal sounds and melodies to enhance emotional and physical well-being. Studies show that structured vocal exercises can improve cognitive function, reduce stress, and enhance social interaction.

Satellite Data and Urban Well-being

Using Copernicus and Galileo satellite data, MOVERE 2.0 identifies urban areas with high environmental stress and limited access to green spaces. This allows us to map optimal locations for chant therapy interventions.
