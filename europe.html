🌍 Child Health Across Europe: Why We Need CosmoPet

🌍 Salute infantile in Europa: perché serve CosmoPet

Childhood overweight and obesity rates vary significantly across Europe. According to WHO and EU data, Italy, Greece, and Romania show the highest incidence.

Movere 2.0 was born in Italy 🇮🇹, where 29% of children are overweight or obese. In Greece 🇬🇷, that rate reaches 38.5%.

Our platform, CosmoPet, is designed to address these numbers by using satellite data and personalized digital therapy. While tested as a pilot in Italy, its modular, scalable model makes it suitable for all countries listed above.

Thanks to the integration of Copernicus Land Monitoring and Galileo GNSS, CosmoPet can adapt to urban environmental contexts in cities like Athens 🇬🇷, Bucharest 🇷🇴, or Paris 🇫🇷.
