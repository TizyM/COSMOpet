This is a simulation of the COSMOpet chatbot interface, designed for use in urban areas under environmental stress.

Features:
  • 📬 Contact nutritionist, psychologist, music therapist, or local coordinator
  • 📍 Personalized interaction via simulated Galileo GNSS
  • 🎯 Triggers based on Copernicus environmental data
  • 📨 GDPR-compliant data collection and consent system
