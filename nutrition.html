🥗 Smart Nutrition and Environmental Health

🥗 Nutrizione Intelligente e Salute Ambientale

The Movere team is based in Sardinia, one of the world's Blue Zones – regions known for longevity, lifestyle, and healthy habits. Two of our researchers come from these communities, which inspired our nutrition concept in CosmoPet.

In CosmoPet, nutrition challenges are gamified and linked to seasonal, local, and healthy food suggestions. In future versions, we aim to cross-reference real-time environmental and agricultural satellite data from Copernicus Land Monitoring Services.

  • 🍏 Children will receive weekly food quests aligned with Mediterranean diet principles.
  • 📍 In the future, Galileo geolocation will adapt food suggestions to their region or city.
  • 🌍 Integration with Copernicus land cover maps could offer insights into food availability and environmental sustainability.

This feature is in early-alpha stage and under development for integration in 2025.
